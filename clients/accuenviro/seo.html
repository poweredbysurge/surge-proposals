Schema Markup Implementation

Schema markup is structured data that tells Google exactly what AccuEnviro does, where it operates, what it costs, and how to contact it — in machine-readable format. Properly implemented schema triggers rich results (stars, FAQs, business info) directly in the search listing, boosting click-through rates before a visitor even reaches the site.
