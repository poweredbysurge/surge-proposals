08 · Paid Media Audit

7 ADS.
1 IDEA.

You are running Meta ads. These seven creatives are proof. But Job Nimbus Marketing launched all seven the same week, with the same angle, same format, same fear: storm damage discount. Running variations of one message is effectively one ad. The account is active. The strategy is flat.

+8
Andromeda needs 15 distinct concepts. You have 1 concept in 7 variations.
What Andromeda Actually Needs

Meta's Andromeda system, the AI backbone that controls ad delivery across Meta's entire network, changed the game for advertisers. Andromeda does not optimize a single message. It learns from creative signals across many distinct concepts and uses those signals to find the right buyer at the right moment. The old advice was 3 to 5 concepts. Post-Andromeda, Meta's own guidance pushes toward 15 or more distinct creative pieces: varied hooks, different formats, multiple angles, and content that speaks to different stages of the buying decision. Running seven variations of one storm damage fear hook is giving Andromeda one data point. That is not enough to find your buyer. That is enough to keep spending your budget on the same audience that already said no.
